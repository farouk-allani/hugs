About HUG

HUG is more than just a token; it’s a movement. Built on the Solana blockchain, HUG leverages Solana’s speed and efficiency to create a seamless experience for users. Our mission is to foster a community of kindness and support, where every transaction contributes to making the world a better place. A portion of every HUG transaction is donated to charitable organizations, ensuring that your participation has a positive impact.

How to Buy HUG

  1. Set up a Solana wallet: Download a wallet like Phantom or Solflare and create an account.
  2. Buy SOL: Purchase SOL on an exchange like Binance, Coinbase, or FTX.
  3. Swap SOL for HUG: Connect your wallet to a decentralized exchange like Raydium, select the HUG token using its contract address: HUG_CONTRACT_ADDRESS, and swap your SOL for HUG.

Important: Always verify the contract address to avoid scams.
